** Initial Setup **
*******************

- Double Click "Setup_Step_1_create_account.bat"
- When the cmd prompt opens, enter in a password, hit enter, retype password hit enter again, close/exit the cmd window.
- Double Click "Start_Expanse.bat"

TADA! You have successfully completed all the steps.

From now on anytime you want to start expanse just double click "start_expanse.bat"
